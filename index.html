What is Enna?

Enna is a media center application. Aiming at simple user interface, Enna is based on the powerfull Enlightenment Foundations Libraries for the user interface and GeeXboX libraries for the media organisation and playback.

Enna features:

  • Browse and listen to your music files,
  • Browse and watch at your videos files,
  • Browse and play slideshow with your photos,
  • Retrieve informations about your medias file on internet,
  • Build a data base with you medias and retrieve informations about your music, video. Such as covers, songs lyrics and more.

Enna is the next user interface of the GeexBoX project and use a lot of powerfull libraries developped by geexbox developpers.
  • libvalhalla : Powerfull data base livrary based on sqlite wich support download of covers, lyrics, informations about your media, retrieve tags in video and music files ...
  • libplayer : The enna media playback backend. Support full control of mplayer, but can be used with vlc or emotion as well !
